About GymBuddy

GymBuddy was built by Master of Applied Computing students at the University of Windsor for COMP-8347 (Internet Applications and Distributed Systems).

Finding a consistent workout partner is hard — schedules clash, fitness levels differ, and motivation fades when you train alone. GymBuddy solves this by matching students and alumni based on their fitness level, goals and availability, and by helping micro-communities form around shared workout interests.

What you can do

Find buddies

Search members by fitness level and interests, and connect with compatible partners.

Book sessions

Create one-on-one or group workout sessions and manage who joins.

Join groups

Open, closed and private fitness groups that meet on a regular schedule.

Rate partners

Build trust with ratings for punctuality, communication and effort.

The team

Built with Python, Django and Bootstrap by a five-member project group. Get in touch through the contact page.
